Common questions.

What exactly do I get from the free analysis?
A custom report showing revenue projections (weekly, monthly, annual), recommended rental rates for each facility type, utilization gaps where demand exists, and a breakdown by facility. It's specific to your district, not a generic template.
Is this really free? What's the catch?
Completely free. No credit card, no commitment, no contract. We provide the analysis because we believe in the value we deliver. If the numbers make sense and you want to explore working together, great. If not, you keep the analysis regardless.
How long does it take to get my analysis?
Most analyses are delivered within 48 hours of submitting your information. For larger districts or more complex facility portfolios, it may take up to 72 hours.
Will someone call me immediately to sell something?
No. We'll send your analysis by email. If you'd like to discuss it, we're happy to schedule a call at your convenience. But we won't hound you. That's not how we operate.
What types of facilities does this cover?
Gyms, athletic fields (football, soccer, baseball), multipurpose rooms, auditoriums, cafeterias, meeting rooms, tennis courts, tracks—essentially any space that could be rented to community groups, sports teams, or organizations.
We already have a rental process. How is this different?
The analysis itself is about understanding what's possible—not replacing what you have. Many districts leave significant revenue on the table simply because they don't have visibility into local demand or appropriate rate structures. This analysis provides that clarity.
